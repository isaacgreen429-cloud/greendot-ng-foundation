Who We Are

Greendot NG Foundation is a registered non-profit organization that offers education, medical support, and community welfare programs across Nigerian states. We operate with transparency and community-first principles.

Our volunteers include teachers, healthcare workers, social workers, and local leaders committed to measurable impact.

Our Core Objectives

Education, Health Support, and Community Welfare — the pillars where we create long-term outcomes.

Education

Scholarships, school supplies, mentorship, and after-school programs to empower children and youth.
